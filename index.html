The State of Indiana, Switzerland County, ss,

In the Switzerland Circuit Court of the term of November in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and fifty-two.

The Grand Jurrors empannelled and sworn in the Switzerland Circuit Court to inquire in and for the body of the County Switzerland upon their oath present:

That Charles R Robinson on the twelfth day of October in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and fifty-two at the county of Switzerland aforesaid, then and there did knowingly and wilfully one horse, did knowingly ride and run what is commonly called a horse race along the public highway in the town of Florence, Switzerland County in the State of Indiana.

Contrary to the form of the statute in such cases made and provided, and against the peace and dignity of the State of Indiana.

                                                    R. P. Moore Pros. Atty.


The State of Indiana, Switzerland County, ss THE STATE OF INDIANA, TO THE SHERIFF OF SAID COUNTY, GREETING:

WE COMMAND YOU, That you take into your custody the body of Charles R. Robinson.

If he is to be found in your bailiwick, and him safely keep; so that you have him before the judges of the Switzerland Circuit Court, at the Court House in Vevay, on the first Monday in May next, to answer unto the State of Indiana on an Indictment for Horse Rcing

and have you then and there this writ. Witness, IRA N. MALIN, clerk of our said Court at Vevay, this 13th day of December 1852.

Ira N. Malin C. S. C. C.
