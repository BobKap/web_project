					The State of Indiana, Switzerland County, ss
THE STATE OF INDIANA, TO THE SHERIFF OF SAID COUNTY, GREETING:
		WE COMMAND YOU, That you take into yourcustodythe body of Charles R. Robinson.
If he is to be found in your bailiwick, and him safely keep; so that you have him before the judges of the switzerland Circuit Court, at the Court House in Vevay, on the first Monday in May next, to answer unto the State of Indiana on an Indictment for Horse Rcing
and have you then and there this writ. Witness, IRA N. MALIN, clerk of our said Court at Vevay, this 13th day of December 1852.

				(signed) Ira N. Malin C. S. C. C.
